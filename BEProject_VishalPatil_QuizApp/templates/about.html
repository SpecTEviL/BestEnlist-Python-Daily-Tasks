About Quizzer

This is a Quiz web app project made as a part of my internship at Best Enlist.
It is a quiz app where we can choose from 3 topics(C++, Python, HTML) and answer some MCQ questions with single correct answer. The Score is displayed on the screen as soon as the question is answered.
